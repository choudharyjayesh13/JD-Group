About

The Belmonte House is a 30-room boutique hotel in Pratap Nagar, Udaipur, set against the Aravalli hills and known for its French-inspired architecture. Air-conditioned rooms come with balconies and city views, while the rooftop is the property's signature — stylish seating around an inviting pool, high above the streets.

More than a hotel, the Belmonte campus is a destination in itself: Illuzion Bar & Lounge, Casablanca Pool Bar & Kitchen and the House of Beauty salon all live at the same address, making it one of east Udaipur's liveliest corners — stay, dine, dance and glow without leaving the building.
