About

The Crown Fort House is the hospitality crown of CPC One – Capital Tower, the landmark mixed-use destination coming up near Geetanjali Hospital, Udaipur. Planned as a 60–80 key business hotel — with an alternative configuration of 30 luxury studios — it brings the drama of Mewar's forts to a contemporary, executive-suite stay.

Developed through Choudhary Properties and Consultancy Private Limited (CPC), the hotel sits atop a full destination: premium retail showrooms, anchor stores, offices and food & beverage outlets, with two levels of basement parking. Guests and members get club amenities and access across the JD network through JD One. Architecture and planning are underway; doors targeted for the 2028–30 window.
