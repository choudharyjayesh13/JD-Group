About

CPC One – Capital Tower is the flagship upcoming project of Choudhary Properties & Consultancy (CPC) — a landmark mixed-use commercial destination on approximately 36,000 sq ft of prime commercial land near Geetanjali Hospital, Udaipur. The development plans roughly 1,20,000 sq ft of saleable area within 1,50,000 sq ft of total construction, including two levels of basement parking and lifestyle amenities.

The proposed mix brings the whole business day under one roof: 24 premium retail showrooms of 624 sq ft each, two anchor retail stores of 3,500 sq ft, 16 premium office units of 600 sq ft, and five to six food & beverage outlets. Crowning the tower is its hospitality component — a 60–80 key business hotel to carry The Crown Fort House name, with an alternative plan for 30 luxury studios of 800 sq ft each.

Retail and office occupants will enjoy club amenities and access across the JD network through JD One. Architecture and planning are underway; doors targeted for the 2028–30 window.
