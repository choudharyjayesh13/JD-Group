About

Choudhary Properties and Consultancy (CPC) is the real estate foundation of JD Group — decades of land dealing, township development and property consultancy across Chittorgarh and Udaipur. From identifying and aggregating land to planning residential colonies and guiding families through purchases, CPC's work is built on local knowledge and long-standing relationships in the Mewar region.

It is the venture from which the group's hotels, restaurants and entertainment businesses grew — the same instinct for location and value, applied first to land. Newly incorporated as a private limited company, CPC today drives the group's biggest projects: The Crown Fort House in Udaipur, the Varanasi resort joint venture and the Delhi NCR commercial build.

Buyers and investors work directly with the team for site selection, documentation and end-to-end transaction support.
